I chose to make the timer easier and more user friendly. There are a lot of different timers across PC, Phones, Tablets, etc., but one thing they all have in common is they like to use the scroll wheel. I don't like that. I would much rather just click the buttons an move on with my day. In this new design you can see how you can just click your hour and minute and go on with your day.
